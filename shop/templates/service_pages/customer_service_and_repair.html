{% trans "CARE FOR YOUR PRODUCT" %}

{% trans "Each of our products is subject to high-quality requirements regarding the quality of production processes, raw materials and manual production. Nonetheless, each piece should be handled with care." %}

{% trans "DAILY CARE" %}

{% trans "Treat your jewelry with love so that you enjoy it for a long time. Avoid prolonged contact with water; remove the jewelry when washing hands or swimming, as both salt and water can damage the stones and the metal." %}

{% trans "To avoid damage, do not wear jewelry when working in the house or garden, as cleaning products, chemicals, and hard objects can damage the coating and leave scratches. Especially cleaning and washing products, perfumes, sprays and skin creams can damage the rhodium or gold coating and dissolve the glue that holds the crystals in their socket. Sweat also has a corrosive effect, so wearing jewelry while exercising is not recommended." %}

{% trans "RULES OF THUMB" %}

{% trans "Keep your jewelry separate in its original packaging if possible or in soft pouches. This will prevent your favorite pieces from scratching one on another." %}

{% trans "Once in a while polish your jewelry with a soft cloth to maintain its lustrous shine. Silver pieces might oxidize; there is no reason to panic, polishing will solve the problem." %}

{% trans "When donning, be sure to put on your jewelry the last, it helps you to match it to your outfit, prevent scratches and you will avoid spraying perfume at your beloved jewelry, unnecessary. When changing, make sure to first take off your jewelry." %}
